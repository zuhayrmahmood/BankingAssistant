# TD Every Day Savings Account

Maps to `AccountType.EVERYDAY_SAVINGS`.

## Monthly Fee
- **$0** — no monthly fee.

## Interest Rate
- **0.010%** flat, on every dollar, calculated daily — effectively negligible. This is a transactional savings account, not a rate-competitive one.

## Included Transactions
- **1 transaction/month** included.
- Additional transactions: **$5.00 each**.
- Unlimited free transfers between the client's own TD accounts (not counted against the transaction limit).

## Who This Fits
This account is best understood as a "parking / transfer hub" account rather than a savings vehicle — the interest rate is too low to matter. A client holding a meaningful balance (say, $5,000+) in Everyday Savings is a strong candidate to be moved into either:
- **TD Growth Savings Account** (not currently modeled in this system) for tiered rates on higher balances, or
- **TD ePremium Savings Account** once balance clears $10,000, or
- An investment product (GIC / mutual fund / TFSA) if the funds are truly idle and not needed for short-term liquidity.

Idle cash sitting in this account is one of the clearest "value creation" signals for a teller conversation.
