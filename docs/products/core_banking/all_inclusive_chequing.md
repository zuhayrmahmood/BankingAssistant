# TD All-Inclusive Banking Plan

Maps to `AccountType.ALL_INCLUSIVE_CHEQUING`.

## Monthly Fee
- **$30.95/month**
- **Waived** with a minimum daily closing balance of **$6,000** every day of the month.

## Senior Discount (age 60+)
- Discounted monthly fee: **$22.45/month**
- Same $6,000 balance waiver applies on top of the discount.

## Included Transactions
- **Unlimited** transactions.

## Other Fees
| Item | Fee |
|---|---|
| Interac e-Transfer — send/request | Free |
| TD & non-TD ATM (Canada) | No fee |
| Foreign ATM (U.S./Mexico) | No fee |
| Foreign ATM (other countries) | No additional TD fee (currency conversion fee still applies) |
| Paper statements | Free |
| Cheque image return | Free |
| Bank drafts | Free |

## Premium Perks Included
This is TD's top chequing tier — the extra fee over Unlimited Chequing buys:
- Small safety deposit box (waived, normally ~$80/year)
- Free personalized cheques (ordered via EasyWeb)
- Free money orders
- Annual fee rebate on one of six eligible TD credit cards (up to ~$139 for primary cardholder, up to ~$75 for one additional cardholder per year)
- No ATM fees anywhere in Canada or the continental US/Mexico

## Who This Fits
Designed for high-balance, high-engagement clients who want a "no fees, no thinking about it" experience and use several banking services (safety deposit box, drafts, cheques) that carry standalone fees on lower tiers. This is the natural top-of-funnel chequing product for primacy conversations with higher-net-worth clients.
