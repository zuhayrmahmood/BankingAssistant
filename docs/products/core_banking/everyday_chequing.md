# TD Every Day Chequing Account

Maps to `AccountType.EVERYDAY_CHEQUING`.

## Monthly Fee
- **$11.95/month**
- **Waived** if you maintain a minimum daily closing balance of **$3,000** every day of the month.

## Senior Discount (age 60+)
- Discounted monthly fee: **$8.20/month**
- Same $3,000 minimum-balance waiver applies — seniors who hold the balance pay $0 either way.
- Existing customers become eligible automatically at the end of their birthday month in which they turn 60.

## Included Transactions
- **25 transactions/month**, plus unlimited public transit purchases.
- Additional transactions beyond the limit: **$1.25 each**.

## Other Fees
| Item | Fee |
|---|---|
| Interac e-Transfer — send/request | Free |
| Non-TD ATM (Canada) | $2.00 |
| Foreign ATM (U.S./Mexico) | $3.00 |
| Foreign ATM (other countries) | $5.00 |
| Overdraft protection | $5 fee + 21% annual interest |
| Paper statements | $2.00/month |
| Cheque image return | $2.00/month |

## Perks
- 0.5 Bonus Stars per $1 spent at participating Starbucks (with linked TD Access Card)
- Mobile cheque deposit, EasyWeb/app access, direct deposit

## Who This Fits
The step up from Minimum Chequing for clients who transact regularly but don't need unlimited transactions and can comfortably park $3,000. A client consistently paying the $1.25 overage here, or one who could easily maintain $4,000, is a candidate to move up to Unlimited Chequing.
