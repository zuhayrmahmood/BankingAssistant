# TD ePremium Savings Account

Maps to `AccountType.EPREMIUM`.

## Monthly Fee
- **$0** — no monthly fee.

## Interest Rate (tiered by balance)
| Balance | Rate |
|---|---|
| $0 – $9,999.99 | 0.000% |
| $10,000+ | 0.450% |

The account earns **no interest at all** below the $10,000 threshold — it only becomes worthwhile once the client can clear that balance. Interest is calculated daily and paid monthly on the last business day.

## Transactions
- Unlimited free online transfers to other TD deposit accounts (via EasyWeb/app).
- Standard transaction fee: **$5.00 each** (this account is not designed for frequent transacting).

## Other Fees
| Item | Fee |
|---|---|
| Non-TD ATM (Canada) | $2.00 |
| Foreign ATM (U.S./Mexico) | $3.00 |
| Foreign ATM (other countries) | $5.00 |

## How to Open
Online (~10 min application), in-branch, or by phone. Joint applicants need at least one eligible account holder. CDIC-insured.

## Who This Fits
This is TD's online-only, higher-rate parking account for clients who don't need branch/cheque access but want a materially better rate than Everyday Savings once they clear $10,000. Relevant for a client with $10,000+ sitting in Everyday Savings (0.010%) — moving it to ePremium is a "quick win" with no fee tradeoff, since both accounts are free. Below $10,000, ePremium offers no advantage over Everyday Savings.
