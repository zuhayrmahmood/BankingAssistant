# TD U.S. Dollar GIC / Term Deposit

Maps to `InvestmentType.GIC` — USD-denominated variant.

## How It Works
Same structure as CAD GICs but denominated and paid out in US dollars, for clients managing USD cash reserves.

## Terms & Minimums
- Terms: 1 day to 5 years
- Minimum investment: **$1,000 USD**
- Cashable or non-cashable options available

## Who This Fits
Clients holding USD cash (snowbirds, cross-border earners, clients with a `us_dollar_visa.md` card or US Daily Interest Chequing balance) who don't need the funds in the near term. Notably these USD rates have recently run *higher* than equivalent CAD non-redeemable GIC rates — worth flagging to a client who might otherwise assume CAD products are always the better return.
