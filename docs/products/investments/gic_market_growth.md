# TD Market Growth GIC

Maps to `InvestmentType.GIC` — equity-linked variant. `GICDetails.interest_rate` is not fixed at issue for this product; it's determined at maturity based on index performance, so model it as a variable/estimated field if this product type is added to the data model.

## How It Works
Combines 100% principal protection with return potential linked to stock market index performance. Client is guaranteed a minimum return (their principal, and often a small guaranteed minimum interest) with upside capped at a stated maximum.

## Terms & Minimums
- Terms: 3 or 5 years
- Minimum investment: **$500**
- Non-redeemable
- Maximum return over the full 5-year term: **55%** (i.e. capped, not compounding indefinitely)

## Who This Fits
For risk-averse clients who still want equity-market upside without downside principal risk — a natural bridge conversation for a client currently in a `gic_non_redeemable.md` product who has expressed interest in "something with more growth potential" but isn't ready for direct mutual fund/equity risk. Good complement to a TFSA/RRSP wrapper conversation since it's a registered-plan-eligible product.
