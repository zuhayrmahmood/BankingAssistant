# TD Special Offer GIC

Maps to `InvestmentType.GIC` — TD's promotional/limited-time rate tier, often the highest short-term rate TD publishes.

## How It Works
Fixed-rate GIC with a promotional rate, available in both cashable (100-day) and non-cashable (14-month, 18-month) structures, with automatic renewal at maturity unless the client instructs otherwise.

## Terms & Minimums
- Terms: 100 days (cashable), 14 months, 18 months (non-cashable)
- Minimum investment: **$500**
- Rate often tiered by deposit amount (e.g. <$10k, $10-50k, $50-100k, >$100k bands)
- Available in TFSA, RRSP, FHSA, RRIF, RESP, and non-registered plans (14/18-month terms)

## Note on Rates
This product's whole purpose is a time-limited promotional rate, so any specific number quickly goes stale — treat this doc as describing the *structure*, and pull the live rate from td.com/ca/en/personal-banking/personal-investing/products/gic/special-offer-rates at the time of the conversation rather than relying on a cached figure.

## Who This Fits
The "act now" GIC — best raised with clients who have cash ready to deploy soon and would respond to a time-boxed rate offer, since the promotional nature creates a natural urgency for the conversation. Good to check current promo terms/dates before referencing an expiry date to a client.
