# TD Cashable GIC

Maps to `InvestmentType.GIC` with `GICDetails.interest_rate` — the liquid variant.

## How It Works
Allows early withdrawal before maturity at a pre-encashment rate (lower than the full-term rate), trading some yield for liquidity.

## Terms & Minimums
- Terms: 100 days, 1 year, 3 years (premium-rate special offer)
- Minimum investment: **$500**

## Indicative Rates (CAD, as of mid-July 2026)
| Term | Rate |
|---|---|
| 1 year | 1.75% |
| 3 years | 2.50% (Premium Rate Special Offer) |

## Who This Fits
For clients who want a better return than a savings account but aren't ready to fully lock in funds — a middle ground between `everyday_savings.md`/`epremium.md` and the higher-yield `gic_non_redeemable.md`. Worth raising for clients with an emergency fund earning near-zero interest who still want same-day-ish access.
