# TD First Class Travel Visa Infinite

Maps to `LiabilityType.VISA` / `PreapprovalProduct.VISA` (top-tier TD Rewards travel card).

## Fees & Rates
- **Annual fee:** $139
- **Purchase interest rate:** 21.99%
- **Cash advance interest rate:** 22.99%

## Rewards (TD Rewards Points)
- **8 points/$1** when booking travel through Expedia For TD.
- **6 points/$1** on groceries, dining, and public transit.
- **2 points/$1** on everything else.

## Eligibility
- Minimum income: **$60,000 personal / $100,000 household**

## Welcome Offer
- Up to $1,300 value, including up to 146,000 TD Rewards Points plus a first-year annual fee rebate — promotional, changes periodically.

## Perks
- Access to 1,200+ airport lounges worldwide via the Visa Airport Companion Program — 4 complimentary visits per membership year, plus dining/retail discounts.
- Travel Medical Insurance up to $2M for the first 21 days (4 days if 65+)
- Flight/Trip Delay Insurance up to $500/person (delay over 4 hours)
- Trip Cancellation/Interruption Insurance up to $1,500/person

## Who This Fits
Clients who book travel (especially through Expedia For TD) rather than flying a specific airline loyalty program — this is TD's answer to Aeroplan Visa Infinite for clients without airline loyalty, and the 6x rate on groceries/dining/transit is stronger than the Aeroplan card's everyday-spend rate. Good alternative pitch for a client not attached to Air Canada specifically.
