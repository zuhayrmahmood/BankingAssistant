# TD Cash Back Visa (no annual fee)

Maps to `LiabilityType.VISA` / `PreapprovalProduct.VISA` (no-fee cash back tier).

## Fees & Rates
- **Annual fee:** $0
- **Purchase interest rate:** 21.99%
- **Cash advance interest rate:** 22.99%

## Rewards
- **1% cash back** on: groceries, gas & EV charging, public transit, recurring bill payments, streaming/digital gaming/media — capped at **$5,000 in annual purchases per category**, then drops to the base rate.
- Lower base rate on everything else (exact base % not published on the comparison page — verify on the card's own page before quoting).

## Eligibility
- No published minimum income requirement.

## Welcome Offer
- Up to $100 value — promotional, changes periodically.

## Who This Fits
Entry-level cash back option for clients who don't spend enough in bonus categories to justify the Infinite's $139 fee, or who don't meet its income requirement. Good "add a credit product" ask for a client with zero TD credit cards, since there's no fee objection to overcome.
