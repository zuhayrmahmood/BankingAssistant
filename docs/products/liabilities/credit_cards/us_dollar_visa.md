# TD U.S. Dollar Visa

Maps to `LiabilityType.VISA` / `PreapprovalProduct.VISA` (USD-denominated card).

## Fees & Rates
- **Annual fee:** $39 USD
- **Purchase interest rate:** 21.99%
- **Cash advance interest rate:** 22.99%

## Rewards
- None published — this card's value proposition is currency, not rewards.

## Who This Fits
Clients who spend regularly in USD — frequent US travelers, snowbirds, or anyone paying USD bills — since it avoids the ~2.5% foreign currency conversion fee charged on CAD cards for USD purchases. Pairs naturally with a `U.S. Daily Interest Chequing Account` or `Borderless Plan` conversation for clients with recurring US-dollar cash flow.
