# TD Aeroplan Visa Platinum

Maps to `LiabilityType.VISA` / `PreapprovalProduct.VISA` (entry-tier Aeroplan travel card).

## Fees & Rates
- **Annual fee:** $89
- **Purchase interest rate:** 21.99%
- **Cash advance interest rate:** 22.99%

## Rewards
- Aeroplan points program (Platinum tier earns at a lower rate than Infinite — verify exact per-category multiplier on TD's card page before quoting, as it wasn't confirmed on the comparison table).

## Eligibility
- No published minimum income requirement (lower bar than the Infinite tier).

## Welcome Offer
- Up to $500 value (≈20,000 points) — promotional, changes periodically.

## Who This Fits
Entry point into the Aeroplan ecosystem for clients who don't meet the $60k/$100k income bar for Aeroplan Visa Infinite, or who want a lower fee. A natural "step-up later" conversation once income/spend grows.
