# TD Rewards Visa (no annual fee)

Maps to `LiabilityType.VISA` / `PreapprovalProduct.VISA` (no-fee TD Rewards card).

## Fees & Rates
- **Annual fee:** $0
- **Purchase interest rate:** 21.99%
- **Cash advance interest rate:** 22.99%

## Rewards
- TD Rewards Points, earned at the base/entry-level rate (exact multiplier not confirmed here — verify on TD's card page).

## Eligibility
- No published minimum income requirement.

## Welcome Offer
- $100 value — promotional, changes periodically.

## Who This Fits
Zero-fee entry point into the TD Rewards points ecosystem — parallel option to TD Cash Back Visa (no-fee) for a client who'd rather redeem for travel/merchandise/statement credit than cash back. Good default recommendation for a client with no credit card and no strong spend pattern yet.
