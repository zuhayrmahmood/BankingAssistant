# TD Minimum Chequing Account

Maps to `AccountType.MINIMUM_CHEQUING`.

## Monthly Fee
- **$3.95/month**
- Unlike the other chequing tiers, this fee **cannot be waived by holding a minimum balance**. There is no balance-based waiver on this account.

## Fee Waiver — Eligible Groups (fee reduced to $0)
The monthly fee is waived entirely (not discounted — full waiver) for account holders who provide proof of belonging to one of these groups:
- Recipients of the **Guaranteed Income Supplement (GIS)**
- **RDSP** (Registered Disability Savings Plan) beneficiaries
- **Indigenous Peoples** of Canada
- **Newcomers to Canada** (waived for their first year only)

Proof of eligibility (e.g., a government letter confirming GIS status) is required to activate the waiver.

## Included Transactions
- **12 transactions/month**, made up of up to 2 full-serve transactions (in-branch or EasyLine phone banking) plus unlimited public transit purchases.
- Additional transactions beyond the limit: **$1.25 each**.

## Other Fees
| Item | Fee |
|---|---|
| Interac e-Transfer — send | Free |
| Interac e-Transfer — request (≤$100) | $0.50 |
| Interac e-Transfer — request (>$100) | $1.00 |
| Non-TD ATM (Canada) | $2.00 |
| Foreign ATM (U.S./Mexico) | $3.00 |
| Foreign ATM (other countries) | $5.00 |
| Overdraft protection | $5 fee + 21% annual interest |
| Paper statements | $2.00/month |

## Senior Discount
No separate age-60+ discount tier exists on this account — seniors instead qualify through the **GIS full-waiver path** above if they receive GIS.

## Who This Fits
This is TD's lowest-cost chequing account, designed for occasional/low-transaction-volume banking rather than day-to-day spending. A client parked in this account who has frequent transactions or is paying the $1.25 overage fee regularly may be a candidate to move up to Everyday Chequing.
