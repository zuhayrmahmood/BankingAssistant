# TD Unlimited Chequing Account

Maps to `AccountType.UNLIMITED_CHEQUING`.

## Monthly Fee
- **$17.95/month**
- **Waived** with a minimum daily closing balance of **$4,000** every day of the month.

## Senior Discount (age 60+)
- Discounted monthly fee: **$11.95/month** (a $6 rebate off the standard fee)
- Same $4,000 balance waiver applies on top of the discount.

## Included Transactions
- **Unlimited** transactions — no per-transaction overage fee regardless of volume.

## Other Fees
| Item | Fee |
|---|---|
| Interac e-Transfer — send/request | Free |
| TD ATM (Canada) | No fee |
| Non-TD ATM (Canada) | No fee (waived on this tier) |
| Foreign ATM (U.S./Mexico) | $3.00 |
| Foreign ATM (other countries) | $5.00 |
| Overdraft protection | $5 fee + 21% annual interest |
| Paper statements | $2.00/month |

## Perks
- No TD (or non-TD, in Canada) ATM fees
- First-year annual fee rebate on one eligible TD credit card (up to ~$139 value)
- 0.5 Bonus Stars per $1 at participating Starbucks
- Mobile cheque deposit, no-hold direct deposit, TD Fraud Alerts

## Who This Fits
Good for clients with frequent transaction volume who can hold $4,000+, or who value free ATM access nationwide. A client here who could comfortably hold $6,000 and wants premium perks (safety deposit box, free cheques/money orders) is a candidate for the All-Inclusive Banking Plan.
