# TD Platinum Travel Visa

Maps to `LiabilityType.VISA` / `PreapprovalProduct.VISA` (mid-tier TD Rewards travel card).

## Fees & Rates
- **Annual fee:** $89
- **Purchase interest rate:** 21.99%
- **Cash advance interest rate:** 22.99%

## Rewards
- TD Rewards Points (lower earn multipliers than First Class Travel Visa Infinite — exact per-category rates not confirmed here, verify on TD's card page).

## Eligibility
- No published minimum income requirement.

## Welcome Offer
- Up to $370 value (≈50,000 points) — promotional, changes periodically.

## Who This Fits
Lower-commitment travel rewards option for clients who want TD Rewards Points without the $139 fee or income bar of First Class Travel Visa Infinite.
