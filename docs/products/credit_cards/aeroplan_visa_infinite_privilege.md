# TD Aeroplan Visa Infinite Privilege

Maps to `LiabilityType.VISA` / `PreapprovalProduct.VISA` (top-tier Aeroplan travel card).

## Fees & Rates
- **Annual fee:** $599
- **Purchase interest rate:** 21.99%
- **Cash advance interest rate:** 22.99%

## Rewards
- Aeroplan points at the highest earn multipliers in TD's card lineup (exact per-category rates not confirmed here — verify on TD's own card page).

## Eligibility
- Minimum income: **$150,000 personal / $200,000 household** — TD's highest income bar.

## Welcome Offer
- Up to $3,000 value (≈85,000 points) — promotional, changes periodically.

## Who This Fits
High-net-worth / high-income clients who travel frequently on Air Canada and would use premium travel perks (expect Maple Leaf Lounge access, higher checked-bag allowances, elevated Aeroplan status boosts — confirm specifics on TD's card page). This is the top-of-funnel ask for a client who already holds Aeroplan Visa Infinite and clearly exceeds the income bar.
