# TD Cash Back Visa Infinite

Maps to `LiabilityType.VISA` / `PreapprovalProduct.VISA` (premium cash back tier).

## Fees & Rates
- **Annual fee:** $139 (first-year rebate frequently offered as a promo)
- **Purchase interest rate:** 21.99%
- **Cash advance interest rate:** 22.99%

## Rewards
- **3% cash back** on: groceries, gas & EV charging, public transit, recurring bill payments, streaming/digital gaming/media — **each category capped at $15,000 in annual purchases**, then drops to the 1% base rate for the rest of the year.
- **1% cash back** on everything else.
- Cash back dollars redeemable any time, don't expire while the account is open and in good standing.

## Eligibility
- Minimum income: **$60,000 personal / $100,000 household**

## Welcome Offer
- 10% cash back in the first 3 months, up to $3,500 in eligible spend (i.e. up to $350) — promotional, changes periodically.

## Perks
- Visa Infinite Concierge (24/7)
- Free Deluxe TD Auto Club Membership
- Mobile Device Insurance
- Travel Medical Insurance up to $2M

## Who This Fits
Clients with meaningful monthly spend in groceries/gas/transit/bills who can clear the income bar and would use the categories enough to offset the $139 fee. Rule of thumb: needs roughly $4,600+/year in bonus-category spend to break even on the fee vs. the no-fee `cash_back_visa.md` card (3% vs 1% earn = 2pp delta; $139 / 2% ≈ $6,950 in category spend to fully offset — lower if the client also values the included insurance/concierge perks).
