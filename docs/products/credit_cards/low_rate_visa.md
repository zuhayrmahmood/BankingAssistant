# TD Low Rate Visa

Maps to `LiabilityType.VISA` / `PreapprovalProduct.VISA` (low interest-rate tier, no rewards).

## Fees & Rates
- **Annual fee:** $25
- **Purchase interest rate:** 12.90% (roughly half of TD's standard 21.99% cards)
- **Cash advance interest rate:** 12.90%

## Rewards
- None — this card trades rewards for a materially lower ongoing interest rate.

## Welcome Offer
- 0% promotional rate on purchases for the first 6 months — promotional, changes periodically.

## Who This Fits
The clear recommendation for any client who **carries a revolving balance** on a standard-rate TD Visa. A client with a VISA liability (see `LiabilityType.VISA` in a `ClientProfile`) showing a persistent balance and TD's standard ~22% interest_rate is paying far more in interest than the $25 fee here would cost — this is one of the most concrete, numbers-backed conversation openers available: "you're paying ~22% interest on a card that doesn't reward you for it; there's a card at roughly half that rate." Not suited for clients who pay their balance in full monthly (they'd lose more in forgone rewards than they'd save in interest they aren't paying anyway).
