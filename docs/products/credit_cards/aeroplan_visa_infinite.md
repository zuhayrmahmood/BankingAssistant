# TD Aeroplan Visa Infinite

Maps to `LiabilityType.VISA` / `PreapprovalProduct.VISA` (mid-tier Aeroplan travel card).

## Fees & Rates
- **Annual fee:** $139
- **Purchase interest rate:** 21.99%
- **Cash advance interest rate:** 22.99%

## Rewards
- **1.5 points/$1** on gas, EV charging, groceries, and direct Air Canada purchases.
- **1 point/$1** on everything else.
- Double-dip: earn points twice by paying with the card *and* scanning Aeroplan membership at 150+ partner brands / 200+ online retailers.
- Points don't expire while the primary cardholder's account is in good standing.
- Up to 8 family members can pool points together.

## Eligibility
- Minimum income: **$60,000 personal / $100,000 household**

## Welcome Offer
- Up to $1,350 value (≈40,000 points) — promotional, changes periodically.

## Perks
- First checked bag free (up to 23kg/50lb) for primary + additional cardholders + up to 8 companions on the same reservation, when travel originates on Air Canada.
- Visa Infinite Concierge (24/7)
- Travel Medical Insurance up to $2M (outside home province/territory)

## Who This Fits
Clients who fly Air Canada regularly or want to build Aeroplan status — the checked-bag perk alone offsets much of the annual fee for a family that flies together even once a year. Good pairing question: "do you or your family travel by Air Canada?"
